Introduction

Breeding 4.0 seeks to enhance breeding efficiency and precision by combining digital and biological technologies to achieve a faster, more efficient and more accurate breeding process. relies on big data, artificial intelligence, machine learning and genome editing technologies to collect, integrate and analyse large amounts of crop genomic, phenotypic and environmental data to predict phenotypic traits in plants and to target breeding on this basis.

We have developed BreedingAI, a user-friendly database that provides breeders and machine learning experts with easy access to genome-phenotype pair data. It provide two types of unstructured data: GVCF(genomic VCF) files and VCF files, along with one type of structured data which is extracted from vcf files by GSCtool, to facilitate research in breeding machine learning.
